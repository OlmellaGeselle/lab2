HOW TO BAKE BLACK FOREST CAKE



Ingredients:

    For the Cake:

    • 2 1/8 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 cups of white sugar
    • 3/4 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
    • 3/4 teaspoon of baking soda
    • 3/4 teaspoon of salt
    • 3 eggs
    • 1 cup of milk

    For the Cream:

    • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
    • 1 tablespoon of vanila extract
    • 2 (20 ounces) cans of pitted sour cherries
    • 1 cup of white sugar
    • 1/4 cup of cornstarch
    • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
    • 3 cups of heavy whipping cream
    • 1/3 cup of confectioner's sugar

Procedures:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9 inch, round, cake pans; cover bottoms with waxed paper.
  3. In a large bowl, combine flour, 2 cups sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and 1 tablespoon vanilla; beat until well blended. Pour batter into prepared pans.
  5. Bake for 35 minutes, or until wooden toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  6. Cool layers in pans on wire racks 10 minutes. Loosen edges, and remove to racks to cool completely.
  7. Drain cherries, reserving 1/2 cup juice. Combine reserved juice, cherries, 1 cup sugar and cornstarch in a 2 quart saucepan.
  8. Cook over low heat until thickened, stirring constantly. Stir in 1 teaspoon vanilla. Cool before using.
  9. Combine whipping cream and confectioner's sugar in a chilled medium bowl. Beat with an electric mixer at high speed until stiff peaks form.
  10. With long serrated knife, split each cake layer horizontally in half. Tear one split layer into crumbs; set aside.
  11. Reserve 1 1/2 cups Frosting for decorating cake; set aside. Gently brush loose crumbs off top and side of each cake layer with pasty brush or hands.
  12. To assemble, place one cake layer on cake plate. Spread with 1 cup frosting; top with 3/4 cup cherry topping.
  13. Top with second cake layer; repeat layers of frosting and cherry topping. Top with third cake layer. Frost side of cake.
  14. Pat reserved crumbs onto frosting on side of cake. Spoon reserved frosting into pastry bag fitted with star decorator tip.
  15. Pipe around top and bottom edges of cake. Spoon remaining cherry topping onto top of cake.
  16. Serve and enjoy.
